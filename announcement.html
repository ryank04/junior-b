School Announcements

Stay updated with the latest news, events, and important information from Blessing Junior School. Check this page regularly for updates about academic schedules, community events, and other important announcements.

Term 1 Opening Date

Feb 15, 2026
Important Academic

We are pleased to announce that Term 1 will begin on Monday, Feb 15, 2026. All students are expected to report to school by 7:30 AM. Please ensure your child has all the required books and supplies.

Parent-teacher meetings will be held during the first week of term. Please check with your child's class teacher for specific dates and times.

Important Reminders:

  • School fees should be paid by the end of the first week
  • All students must be in proper school uniform
  • PE kits should be brought on the first day

Annual Sports Day

June 5, 2026
Event Sports

Our Annual Sports Day will be held on Friday, June 16th, 2026. This year's theme is "Healthy Bodies, Healthy Minds." All parents and guardians are invited to attend and cheer for our young athletes.

Events will include track races, football matches, traditional games, and a special parents' race. Please ensure your child comes dressed in their PE uniform.

Schedule:

  • 8:00 AM - Opening Ceremony
  • 9:00 AM - Track Events Begin
  • 11:00 AM - Football Matches
  • 1:00 PM - Traditional Games
  • 2:30 PM - Parents' Race & Closing

Health Check-up Camp

April 10, 2026
Health Community

In partnership with Luweero District Hospital, we will be hosting a free health check-up camp on Saturday, May 6th, 2026 from 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM. Services will include:

  • General health screenings
  • Dental check-ups
  • Eye examinations
  • Nutritional assessments
  • Immunizations for children
  • Blood pressure and sugar tests for adults

This event is open to all students and community members. No registration required. We encourage all parents to bring their children for these important health services.

New Library Opening

April 15, 2026
Facilities Academic

We are excited to announce the opening of our new school library, made possible by generous donations from our partners. The library features over 1,000 books, reading corners, and computer stations.

The official opening ceremony will be held on Friday, April 28th, 2026 at 10:00 AM. Special guests include the District Education Officer and our library donors.

Students will begin using the library starting Monday, May 1st. Each class will have scheduled library time, and students will be able to borrow books for home reading.
