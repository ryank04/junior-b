Meet Our Director

Mr. Tugume Eric

Founder & Director of Blessing Junior School

Our founder, Mr. Tugume Eric, recognized the critical need for accessible,

quality education in our community. With limited resources but boundless determination,

he established this school with the firm belief that every child deserves access to

excellent education that nurtures their talents and prepares them for a successful future.
